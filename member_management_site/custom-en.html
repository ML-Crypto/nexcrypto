FAQ

Common questions about custom development services

How long does custom development take?

Development time depends on feature complexity. Simple adjustments may take just a few days, while complete cross-platform integration systems require more time. We'll provide an estimated timeline during the consultation phase.

How is custom pricing calculated?

Pricing is based on feature requirements, development complexity, and time. We provide detailed quotes after confirming requirements to ensure transparency. Feel free to discuss your budget and needs first.

Can you integrate exchanges not currently supported?

Yes. As long as the exchange provides relevant APIs, we can develop the integration. Please provide the exchange name you need, and we'll evaluate technical feasibility.

I have an idea but not sure if it's possible?

Feel free to contact us anytime! We'll evaluate the technical feasibility of your idea and provide suggestions and alternatives. Many innovative features started from customer ideas.

Is there support after development is complete?

Yes, we provide comprehensive after-sales technical support including system maintenance, troubleshooting, and feature adjustments. Specific support details will be clearly stated during collaboration.

Can I add features to existing Discord/LINE systems?

Yes. If you've already purchased our standard solutions, you can add custom features on top. We'll evaluate the existing system architecture and provide the best expansion plan.
